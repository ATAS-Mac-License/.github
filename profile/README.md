## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of ATAS for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move ATAS.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.

## Overview

<a href="#">ATAS for macOS</a> is aimed at traders who work close to the order book and care about how liquidity and flow evolve tick by tick. Rather than presenting only clean price lines, the platform surfaces depth, volume and tape-derived cues that reveal where participants are truly engaging. This style of interface invites users to think in terms of auction behaviour, absorption and aggression instead of generic candles. Over time, this can reshape how risk and opportunity are evaluated intraday.

Depth ladders, footprint views, heatmaps or cluster tools are arranged to make reading flow feel as natural as reading a chart. Users can adjust scales, colour schemes and aggregation to align visuals with their trading tempo. Highlighting of key events—such as size appearing at key levels, sudden surges of market orders or pullbacks in resting liquidity—helps focus attention where it matters. With <a href="#">fine-grained customisation</a>, traders can tune the interface to their personal style.

On macOS, ATAS is designed to run as part of a busy trading desktop without dominating resources. Window layouts can be snapped to specific monitors, while notifications and sound cues complement, rather than overwhelm, the broader environment. The combination of detailed order-flow insight and stable interaction makes the platform a durable companion for those who build their edge around microstructure.

- Integration alongside execution platforms and risk tools  
- Customisable alerts and cues based on flow conditions  
- Stable performance during intense market periods
